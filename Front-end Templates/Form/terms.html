1. Service Description

Tech Care is a digital platform that connects patients, doctors, nurses, pharmacists, and blood donors. The platform enables:

  • For patients: Search and book doctors or nurses for home visits, order medications from nearby pharmacies (cash or online payment).
  • For doctors & nurses: Create a verified profile, set consultation fees, and provide home visits.
  • For pharmacists: Synchronize a limited catalog of available medications; users can reserve or request delivery.
  • Blood donation: Patients can request specific blood types; potential donors matching the criteria receive notifications.

Tech Care acts solely as an intermediary. All medical services are provided by licensed independent professionals.
